Dataset Description: Molde Tourism Data
This dataset contains tourism-related information for the Molde region, organized across 122
observations and 12 variables. It integrates climate conditions, tourist activity, infrastructure
usage, and seasonal indicators, making it suitable for exploratory data analysis (EDA) to
understand tourism trends and influencing factors.
Key Features
Neighborhood (categorical) – The area within Molde where data was recorded (e.g.,
Sentrum).
Month (categorical) – Calendar month of observation (Jan–Dec).
Season (categorical) – Season classification (Winter, Spring, Summer, Autumn).
Tourists (integer) – Number of tourists recorded in that period.
Rainfall_mm (float) – Average monthly rainfall (in millimeters). (5 missing values)
Avg_Temp_C (float) – Average monthly temperature (°C). (5 missing values)
Hotel_Occupancy_% (float) – Percentage of hotel occupancy.
Events_Count (integer) – Number of events held in the area.
Fjord_View_Rating (float) – A rating (1–5) of fjord visibility and attractiveness. (5 missing
values)
Ferry_Traffic (integer) – Number of ferry movements during the period.
High_Season (categorical) – Indicator of whether the period is high or low tourist season.
Source (categorical) – Source of the data (e.g., VisitMolde, fictitious).
