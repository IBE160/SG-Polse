UX Design Direction Showcase - ibe160 Chatbot

Explore different visual and interaction approaches for the ibe160 Course-FAQ Chatbot's key screens. Focus is on the Student Chatbot Interface.

1. Minimal Chat

**Personality:** Efficient & Focused
**Best for:** Direct interaction, content-first
**Key Characteristics:** Single column, spacious, text-focused.

Hello BIP! How can I help you with IBE160 today?
What's the deadline for Assignment 3?
The deadline for Assignment 3 is November 20, 2025, at 11:59 PM. (Source: IBE160 Syllabus, Page 5)
**Layout:** Single column, chat history centered.
**Density:** Spacious.
**Navigation:** Implicit (within chat).
**Primary action prominence:** High (send button).

2. Structured Chat with Sidebar

**Personality:** Organized & Contextual
**Best for:** Information-rich environments, quick access to resources
**Key Characteristics:** Two-column, clear separation, structured content.
